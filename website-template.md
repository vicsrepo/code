# Website Template

## Home Page

**Header Section:**

* **Logo**: Centered 3D-rendered "ADAPPT" logo with glowing electric blue edges.
* **Navigation**: Horizontal menu with links to Home, Services, Portfolio, About, Blog, and Contact. Smooth animated hover effects with an electric blue underline.

**Hero Section:**

* **Background**: Dark gradient with subtle texture.
* **Tagline**: "Elevating Your Photography Business to New Heights" in a clean sans-serif font.
* **Call-to-Action**: "Get Started" button styled with electric blue accents and a drop shadow effect.

## Services Section

**Grid Layout:**

* **Web Development**: Brief description with translucent code brackets and a mockup image of a responsive website. Call-to-action: "View Our Work."
* **Design & Branding**: Showcase logos and color palettes with typography samples. Call-to-action: "Discover Our Approach."
* **Marketing**: Icons representing social media platforms and an SEO graph showcasing results. Call-to-action: "Boost Your Visibility."
* **Creative Tools**: Visuals of photo book templates alongside promotional materials. Call-to-action: "Explore Templates."

## Portfolio Section

**Image Carousel:**

* Full-width image carousel displaying high-quality photos from previous projects with subtle transition effects and the ability to click for more details.

## About Section

**Two-Column Layout:**

* Left column: Professional photo of the team or founder, encapsulating creativity.
* Right column: Brief introduction describing ADAPPT's mission and vision for empowering wedding photographers.

## Blog Section

**Featured Articles:**

* Grid layout displaying the latest blog posts, with featured images, titles, and short descriptions. Each article card will have a "Read More" button.

## Contact Section

**Simple Form:**

* Name, Email, Message fields in a centered form layout with electric blue highlights on input fields. A "Submit" button styled with rounded edges.
* Maps integration showing the agency location.

## Footer Section

* **Links**: Quick links to important pages (Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Social Media Channels).
* **Newsletter Signup**: Simple form to subscribe to updates and tips for photographers.

## Design Elements

* **Color Palette**: Dark background with monochrome elements (greys and whites) contrasted with electric blue accents.
* **Typography**: Clean sans-serif fonts for headers and body text ensuring readability. Alternating font weights for hierarchy.
* **Icons and Graphics**: Minimalistic and monochrome with electric blue highlights, maintaining a cohesive aesthetic throughout the site.
* **Spacing**: Generous use of white space provides a clean and uncluttered feel to enhance user experience.

## Final Touches

* **Responsive Design**: Ensure the website is mobile and tablet-friendly with flexible grid layouts.
* **Loading Animation**: Subtle loading animations when transitioning between sections, providing a smooth user experience.

This website template for ADAPPT combines modern design elements with a clear focus on the business offerings tailored for wedding photographers, establishing a professional and inviting online presence.
